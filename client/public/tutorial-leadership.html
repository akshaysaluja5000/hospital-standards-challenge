Leadership & Compliance Officer Guide

Everything you need to manage your team, monitor readiness, and walk into survey week with confidence.

Director · CEO · Admin Hospital & ASC Joint Commission · AAAHC · CMS
Getting started
1
Create your account and select facility type
Go to accreditationready.ai and sign up. During registration, select your organization type — Hospital (Joint Commission standards) or ASC (AAAHC standards). This determines which entire content library your organization trains on. You cannot switch modules after setup without contacting support.
2
Set up multi-factor authentication CEO+ required
If your account is assigned a CEO, Admin, or Super Admin role, MFA is mandatory before you can access the Leadership Console. Go to Profile → MFA Setup after your first login and link an authenticator app. Leadership Console access remains blocked until MFA is verified each session.
3
Open the Leadership Console
From your dashboard header, click the green Leadership Console button. This hub contains your four management tools: Executive Readiness Report, User Management, Guided Education Oversight, and AI Leadership Coach.
Learner→ Educator→ Director→ CEO→ Admin→ Super Admin
Director and above can access the Leadership Console. CEO and above additionally require MFA.
User management
4
Monitor learners and assign leadership roles
In User Management, every registered learner is listed with their total XP, current streak, questions answered today, and overall accuracy rate. From this table you can promote any user to a leadership role — Educator, Director, CEO, Admin, or Super Admin — using the role dropdown next to their name.
Director+ access
Leadership roles control console visibility. Educators see only their department. Directors and above see the full organization. Be deliberate when assigning roles — CEO accounts require MFA, which will be prompted on their next login.
5
Review the audit log Admin+ only
Admin and Super Admin accounts have access to a full Audit Log inside User Management. Every leadership role change and key facility-level action is recorded with a timestamp and the acting user's name. This trail is available for surveyor review on request.
Readiness monitoring
6
Read the Executive Readiness Report
The Executive Readiness Report shows facility-wide readiness scores, weekly trend charts (questions assigned vs. completed), and the status of all active Guided Education plans. Filter by time period or risk level. Export the full report as CSV or PDF for board meetings or survey binders.
7
Use the AI Leadership Coach AI-powered
The AI Leadership Coach reads your org's performance data and generates plain-language readiness summaries, highlights staff performance outliers, and recommends specific chapters to focus on before your survey window. It does not store or transmit patient data — analysis is based solely on quiz performance and engagement metrics.
Guided education & remediation
8
Understand when remediation plans are created
A Guided Education plan is created only when a learner scores below the passing threshold on the Final Assessment. Diagnostic quizzes, practice quizzes, and Deep Dive Tracers never trigger remediation — regardless of score. This keeps learners from feeling penalized during routine practice.
Remediation trigger: Final Assessment + score below passing threshold.
No remediation: Diagnostic quiz · Practice quiz · Deep Dive Tracer · Flashcard review.
9
Monitor plans through Guided Education Oversight
Every active remediation plan appears in Guided Education Oversight (Leadership Console). Each plan shows: learner name, failed chapter, quiz score, days active, days overdue, and current status. Plans move through four stages:
Assigned In Progress Completed Verified
10
Verify completed plans
Once a learner marks their plan complete, it requires a supervisor sign-off to reach Verified status. Track all plans pending verification in the Guided Education Oversight table. The complete audit trail — assignment, completion, and verification timestamps — is available for surveyor review.
