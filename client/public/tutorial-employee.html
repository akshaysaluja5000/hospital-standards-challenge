Employee & Staff Training Guide

How to use Accreditation Ready for daily training — so you're prepared to answer surveyor questions confidently, year-round.

All clinical & non-clinical staff Hospital & ASC ~10 min/day
First login
1
Create your account and pick your facility type
Go to accreditationready.ai and sign up with your work email. Select your organization type when prompted — Hospital or ASC (Ambulatory Surgery Center). This loads the correct accreditation content for your workplace:
🏥 Hospital
Joint Commission standards, role-based chapter assignments
🔬 ASC
AAAHC standards, all chapters available to every staff member
2
Select your role Hospital users only
If you are a hospital user, you will be prompted to choose your role — for example OR Circulating Nurse, Scrub Tech, OR Manager, or SPD Technician. Your role determines which training chapters appear on your dashboard. ASC users skip this step — all AAAHC chapters are visible to everyone.
You can change your role anytime from the dashboard using the Change button next to your role name. If your role has cross-department access, a toggle lets you switch between "my chapters only" and "show all."
Start with the diagnostic
3
Take the Diagnostic Quiz Do this first
The Diagnostic Quiz is your starting benchmark. It is 25 questions (~10 minutes) and maps where your knowledge gaps are before you begin training. For hospital users it covers your role's key standards. For ASC users it spans 6 AAAHC chapters. Your score is saved — retake it anytime later to see how much you've improved.
The Diagnostic never triggers a remediation plan — no matter how you score. It is purely for your own awareness.
Daily training
4
Practice quizzes on each chapter
Each chapter card on your dashboard has a Practice Quiz button. Questions are drawn from a larger pool each session so you get fresh questions — not the same ones on repeat. Hospital users see chapter cards filtered to their role. ASC users see chapters grouped into Universal Standards (apply to every ASC) and Selective Standards (apply when your facility offers that service).
5
Study with flashcards (spaced repetition)
Each chapter also has a Flashcards button that opens a spaced-repetition study mode. Cards are scheduled to reappear just before you'd normally forget them. When flashcards are due, a purple banner appears at the top of your dashboard — click Review Now to work through them and keep your streak strong.
6
Search any topic for an AI Quick Quiz AI-powered
The search bar at the top of your dashboard searches every standard, chapter, and concept in your module. Find a result and click Quick Quiz — the AI generates 5 focused questions on that exact topic on the spot. This is useful before a morning huddle, after a policy meeting, or any time a specific surveyor topic comes up.
7
Hit your daily goal and maintain your streak
Your sidebar shows a Daily Goal progress bar (default 5 questions per day) and a streak flame. Every day you meet your goal extends your streak. Your XP total, current streak, and best-ever streak are visible to your supervisor and appear on the facility Leaderboard.
🔥 Current streak ⚡ Total XP 📈 Best streak
Deeper study tools
8
Run a Deep Dive Tracer
The Deep Dive Tracer simulates the way a surveyor traces a patient case through your department — from intake through discharge. It identifies which standards are evaluated at each handoff point and flags where deficiencies commonly appear. Available from each chapter card (for ASC) or from the Deep Dive menu in the navigation.
The Deep Dive Tracer never triggers a remediation plan — it is a simulation tool for learning, not a graded assessment.
9
Reference the Compliance Handbook
The Compliance Handbook is a full, searchable reference for every standard in your module. Open it from the dashboard or from any chapter card using the "Read in Handbook" button (ASC). Use it to look up specific requirements, documentation expectations, and plain-language definitions — especially helpful when writing or reviewing a policy.
Final assessment
10
Take the Final Assessment when ready
The Final Assessment is 25 advanced questions. For hospital users it unlocks once you have met eligibility across your assigned chapters. For ASC users it is always available as a post-test. Your score is compared side-by-side with your Diagnostic baseline to show how much you have grown since you started.
Important: Failing the Final Assessment below the passing threshold will create a Guided Education (remediation) plan that your supervisor can see and must sign off on. Practice quizzes and diagnostics never trigger a remediation plan — only the Final Assessment does.
Pass → survey-ready ✓ Fail → remediation plan assigned
Your profile & sidebar
11
Manage settings and track your focus areas
Click the gear icon in the dashboard header to reach your Profile. Here you can set your daily question goal, toggle night mode, and update your account details. In your dashboard sidebar, the Focus Areas panel automatically highlights any chapters where your quiz scores have fallen below 80% — with a one-click Retry button for each.
