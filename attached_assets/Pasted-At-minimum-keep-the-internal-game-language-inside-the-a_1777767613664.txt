gold/orange only for assessment or high-value actions.

Role-based readiness checks staff actually complete

Instead of:

“Pre-built Joint Commission tracer content”

Tracer-style questions modeled on real survey workflows

Make the dashboard proof-oriented

“Top risk chapters this month”

“Units with lowest readiness”

“Most-missed standards”

“Completion by role”

That would make the dashboard feel like a management tool instead of a learning interface with a sidebar.

Most products give standards and questions; yours is getting closer to saying, “Here is where real facilities screw up.” Keep pushing that.

I also like the final assessment / posttest / handbook / deep dive tracer cluster. That grouping makes sense and suggests a learning path rather than random modules.

more premium typography/branding,
