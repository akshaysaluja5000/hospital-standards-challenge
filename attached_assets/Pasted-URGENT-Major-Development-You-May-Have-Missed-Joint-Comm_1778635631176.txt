URGENT — Major Development You May Have Missed
Joint Commission announced on June 30, 2025 a complete overhaul of its standards titled "Accreditation 360: The New Standard" — which took effect January 1, 2026. Anesthesia Patient Safety Foundation
Here's what that means for AccreditationReady.ai:
The restructuring includes a complete overhaul of the standards numbering system and elements of performance, making prior versions of the standards vastly different from the newly published standards. Anesthesia Patient Safety Foundation
Specifically:
The Environment of Care (EC) and Life Safety (LS) chapters have been consolidated into a single, unified chapter titled "Physical Environment." Previously these two chapters contained 44 standards with 396 elements of performance — now reduced by 46-48%. Anesthesia Patient Safety Foundation
In total, JC removed 714 requirements from the hospital accreditation program. Anesthesia Patient Safety Foundation

What This Means for Your Platform
Every module built using old standard numbers — EC.02.03.01, LS chapters, old EM numbering — may now reference deprecated standard IDs. The content substance is largely the same, but:

Old standard numbers are no longer cited in surveys
The chapter structure your platform uses may not match what surveyors reference in 2026
A compliance officer reviewing your platform will immediately notice outdated numbering

The good news: JC has noted it will not cite hospitals for references to old standards as long as the requirement is still being met — but that grace period won't last forever. Anesthesia Patient Safety Foundation

Also Critical — Standards Are Now FREE and Public
As part of Accreditation 360, Joint Commission has made all accreditation and certification standards publicly available and searchable online — no login or account required. ProQuest
This means Akshay can access the complete current 2026 standard structure right now at jointcommission.org/en-us/standards/public-standards — for free.
